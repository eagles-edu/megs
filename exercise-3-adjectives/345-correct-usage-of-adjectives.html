Our house is kind of yellow. → We have a ___ house.

He often acts like a child. → He often acts ___.

We enjoyed the drums' rhythm. → We enjoyed the ___ drums.

She always acts with courtesy. → She is always ___.

She always acts with courtesy. → She is always ___.

She always acts with courtesy. → She is always courteous .

Tara is a beauty. → Tara is beautiful .

The journey was full of peril. → The journey was perilous .

I was very ____-minded and forgot my keys on the table.

I was very absent -minded and forgot my keys on the table.

Helen has found a ten-_____ bill in the street.

Helen has found a ten-_____ note in the street.

Helen has found a ten- dollar bill in the street.

Tom is fond of thirty-_____ walks in the park.

Tom is fond of thirty-_____ walks in the park.

Tom is fond of thirty- minute walks in the park.

I don’t like this second-_____ phone. It doesn’t work well.

I don’t like this second-_____ phone. It doesn’t work well.

I don’t like this second- hand phone. It doesn’t work well.

A two-_____ old boy fell down and cut his left knee.

A two-_____ old boy fell down and cut his left knee.

A two- old old boy fell down and cut his left knee.

We live in a five-_____ building in the center of the city.

We live in a five-_____ building in the center of the city.

We live in a five- story building in the center of the city.

This coat is _____-fashioned, I don’t like it at all.

This coat is _____-fashioned, I don’t like it at all.

This coat is old -fashioned, I don’t like it at all.

My friends stayed in a five-_____ hotel in Paris and were very pleased.

My friends stayed in a five-_____ hotel in Paris and were very pleased.

My friends stayed in a five- star hotel in Paris and were very pleased.
