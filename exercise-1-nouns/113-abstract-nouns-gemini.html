1. The library is a completely silent place. The library is a place of complete _____.

1. The library is a completely silent place. The library is a place of complete _____.

1. The library is a place of complete silence.

2. The attic is a dirty room. The attic is filled with ______.

2. The attic is a dirty room. The attic is filled with ______.

2. The attic is filled with dirt.

3. She was a famous singer. She first won _____ as a singer.

3. She was a famous singer. She first won _____ as a singer.

3. She first won fame as a singer.

4. I heard the joyful news. I could have shouted with _____.

4. I heard the joyful news. I could have shouted with _____.

4. I could have shouted with joy.

5. The lake is deep. What is the _____ of the lake?

5. The lake is deep. What is the _____ of the lake?

5. What is the depth of the lake?

6. The door is three feet wide. The ______ of the door is three feet.

6. The door is three feet wide. The ______ of the door is three feet.

6. The width of the door is three feet.

7. That really is a high wall. I think it is at least 3 metres in _____.

7. That really is a high wall. I think it is at least 3 metres in _____.

7. I think it is at least 3 metres in height.

8. I hope you won't think me rude if I leave now. Excuse my _____ but I have to leave now.

8. I hope you won't think me rude if I leave now. Excuse my _____ but I have to leave now.

8. Excuse my rudeness but I have to leave now.

9. Why is she so jealous? She'd never felt _____ before.

9. Why is she so jealous? She'd never felt _____ before.

9. She'd never felt jealousy before.

10. They were polite to accept the food. They accepted the food out of _____.

10. They were polite to accept the food. They accepted the food out of _____.

10. They accepted the food out of politeness.

11. Jack and Jill enjoy being together. Jack and Jill find _____in being together.

11. Jack and Jill enjoy being together. Jack and Jill find _____in being together.

11. Jack and Jill find enjoyment in being together.

12. You made a careless mistake. It was a mistake of _____.

12. You made a careless mistake. It was a mistake of _____.

12. It was a mistake of carelessness.

13. Mike did it because he believed it would work. Mike did it in the _____ that it would work.

13. Mike did it because he believed it would work. Mike did it in the _____ that it would work.

13. Mike did it in the belief that it would work.

14. You and I agree to always tell each other the truth about everything. You and I have an _____ to always tell each other the truth about everything.

14. You and I agree to always tell each other the truth about everything. You and I have an _____ to always tell each other the truth about everything.

14. You and I have an agreement to always tell each other the truth about everything.

15. She feels proud to wear her new policewoman's uniform. She wears her new policewoman's uniform with _____.

15. She feels proud to wear her new policewoman's uniform. She wears her new policewoman's uniform with _____.

15. She wears her new policewoman's uniform with pride.

16. The patient refused to accept the recommended treatment. It was a _____ by the patient to accept the recommended treatment.

16. The patient refused to accept the recommended treatment. It was a _____ by the patient to accept the recommended treatment.

16. It was a refusal by the patient to accept the recommended treatment.

17. Many people doubt his statement is accurate. They begin to question the _____ of his statement.

17. Many people doubt his statement is accurate. They begin to question the _____ of his statement.

17. They begin to question the accuracy of his statement.

18. Sometimes we make mistakes because we are ignorant. Sometimes our mistakes are due to _____.

18. Sometimes we make mistakes because we are ignorant. Sometimes our mistakes are due to _____.

18. Sometimes our mistakes are due to ignorance.

19. He was trying hard not to let others know he was impatient. He was trying hard not to show his _____.

19. He was trying hard not to let others know he was impatient. He was trying hard not to show his _____.

19. He was trying hard not to show his impatience.

20. It is extremely important that you arrive on time. It is of the utmost _____ that you arrive on time.

20. It is extremely important that you arrive on time. It is of the utmost _____ that you arrive on time.

20. It is of the utmost importance that you arrive on time.
