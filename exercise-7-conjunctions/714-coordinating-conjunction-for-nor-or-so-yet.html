7.1.4 Coordinating Conjunction (for ,nor, or, so, yet)

  • 7.1.3 Coordinating Conjunction (or, so)
  • 7.2.1 Subordinating Conjunction

Use one the following coordinating conjunctions to fill each blank: for , nor , or , so , yet .

Click to check out your answers.

1. Walk quickly _____ you will be late.

1. Walk quickly _____ you will be late.

1. Walk quickly or you will be late.

2. It looks a genuine piece of antique, _____ it's a fake.

2. It looks a genuine piece of antique, _____ it's a fake.

2. It looks a genuine piece of antique, yet it's a fake.

3. We avoid the library on Sundays, _____ it's always crowded.

3. We avoid the library on Sundays, _____ it's always crowded.

3. We avoid the library on Sundays, for it's always crowded.

4. Jack can't come over tomorrow and _____ can Jill.

4. Jack can't come over tomorrow and _____ can Jill.

4. Jack can't come over tomorrow and nor can Jill.

5. I heard a noise _____ I turned the light on.

5. I heard a noise _____ I turned the light on.

5. I heard a noise so I turned the light on.

6. It's almost time for the train to depart but we have not bought our tickets _____.

6. It's almost time for the train to depart but we have not bought our tickets _____.

6. It's almost time for the train to depart but we have not bought our tickets yet .

7. They handed him to the police, _____ he was caught stealing from a woman.

7. They handed him to the police, _____ he was caught stealing from a woman.

7. They handed him to the police, for he was caught stealing from a woman.

8. They believed the dark figure they saw that night was neither a ghost _____ a human.

8. They believed the dark figure they saw that night was neither a ghost _____ a human.

8. They believed the dark figure they saw that night was neither a ghost nor a human.

9. She said she was very angry, _____ she was smiling.

9. She said she was very angry, _____ she was smiling.

9. She said she was very angry, yet she was smiling.

10. We told him to stop reading and turn off the light _____ we can sleep.

10. We told him to stop reading and turn off the light _____ we can sleep.

10. We told him to stop reading and turn off the light so we can sleep.

11. Those thugs must be arrested _____ they will not continue to terrorize the residents here.

11. Those thugs must be arrested _____ they will not continue to terrorize the residents here.

11. Those thugs must be arrested so they will not continue to terrorize the residents here.

12. Do you know if Michael is working _____ on leave today?

12. Do you know if Michael is working _____ on leave today?

12. Do you know if Michael is working or on leave today?

13. "We would have neither fruits _____ ice-cream for dessert," we told the waitress.

13. "We would have neither fruits _____ ice-cream for dessert," we told the waitress.

13. "We would have neither fruits nor ice-cream for dessert," we told the waitress.

14. You asked for more time, _____ you were not able to complete the job on time.

14. You asked for more time, _____ you were not able to complete the job on time.

14. You asked for more time, yet you were not able to complete the job on time.

15. The injured boy must receive medical attention at once _____ he will die.

15. The injured boy must receive medical attention at once _____ he will die.

15. The injured boy must receive medical attention at once or he will die.

16. "Lower your voice _____ the others will not know of our plan."

16. "Lower your voice _____ the others will not know of our plan."

16. "Lower your voice so the others will not know of our plan."

17. His face is slightly swollen, _____ he got into a fight with someone.

17. His face is slightly swollen, _____ he got into a fight with someone.

17. His face is slightly swollen, for he got into a fight with someone.

18. After the argument he never saw her again, _____ did he miss her.

18. After the argument he never saw her again, _____ did he miss her.

18. After the argument he never saw her again, nor did he miss her.

19. None of us could tell if the Panda was male _____ female.

19. None of us could tell if the Panda was male _____ female.

19. None of us could tell if the Panda was male or female.

20. We felt like we had arrived, _____ according to the driver we're still five miles from the city.

20. We felt like we had arrived, _____ according to the driver we're still five miles from the city.

20. We felt like we had arrived, yet according to the driver we're still five miles from the city.
